What is TennisCoin?

TennisCoin (TENCO) represents more than just a cutting-edge utility token—it embodies a transformative force poised to revolutionize the tennis world. By investing in TennisCoin, you’re not merely participating in a financial transaction; you’re making a profound and tangible impact on the lives of young athletes. These unique benefits offer you an unparalleled way to engage with the sport you love, while also contributing to its future. By holding TennisCoin, you’re not just a spectator; you’re an integral part of the tennis community, actively shaping the sport’s evolution and enjoying a diverse array of memorable and valuable experiences. TennisCoin is more than a token; it’s your gateway to becoming a central figure in the vibrant world of tennis, combining your passion for the sport with the opportunity to make a lasting difference.

The Vision of TennisCoin

Our vision is clear, yet deeply ambitious: to break down the barriers that prevent children and teenagers from all walks of life from accessing the world of tennis, no matter their economic or social circumstances. We believe that talent should never be restricted by financial limitations or lack of resources, and that every young person deserves the chance to chase their dreams on the court. At the core of this transformative mission is TennisCoin, a powerful tool designed to create real, lasting change. Through TennisCoin, we are able to channel essential financial resources directly into youth development programs, offering not only the training and mentorship that young athletes need but also providing scholarships to ensure that no promising talent is left behind due to financial hardship. But we don’t stop there. TennisCoin also supports the building and improvement of tennis infrastructure in underserved communities, ensuring that children and teenagers have access to proper facilities where they can train, compete, and grow. Whether it’s refurbishing courts, providing equipment, or funding coaching programs, TennisCoin is committed to creating sustainable, community-based solutions that help young players flourish. Our goal isn’t just to open the door to tennis—it’s to empower the next generation to walk through it, equipped with the skills, confidence, and opportunities they need to succeed. With TennisCoin, we’re leveling the playing field and ensuring that tennis becomes a sport where talent, passion, and hard work are the only requirements for success, not the ability to pay.

TennisCoin Tokenomics

1. Token Distribution

The distribution of TennisCoin is designed to ensure balanced and sustainable growth for the project. Tokens will be distributed as follows:

  • 50% - Public Sale: During the public sale phase, 50% of the total tokens will be made available for purchase. This sale will help us raise the necessary funds to start and support youth tennis programs.
  • 20% - Airdrop: To incentivize participation and build a strong, engaged community, 20% of the tokens will be distributed through airdrops to active community members. This will help increase the project's visibility and involve new supporters.
  • 10% - Reserved for the Team: 10% of the tokens will be reserved for team members as an incentive for their commitment and dedication to the project's development. These tokens will be subject to a vesting period to ensure project stability.
  • 20% - Future Development: 20% of the tokens will be allocated for the future development of the project, including platform enhancements, service expansion, and exploring new opportunities. This fund will ensure continuous growth and innovation for TennisCoin.

2. Token Utilization

TennisCoin can be used in various ways within our ecosystem, with the goal of supporting and promoting youth tennis. Here are some of the primary uses:

  • Access to Exclusive Services: TennisCoin holders can use tokens to access online tennis lessons, register for tournaments and competitions, and access exclusive educational content.
  • Support for Youth Programs: The funds raised through tokens will be used to finance youth development programs, scholarships, and infrastructure for communities in need. Every TennisCoin directly contributes to the growth of young tennis talent.
  • Community Reward Program: Active users on our platform can earn TennisCoin as a reward for their participation and engagement. The earned tokens can be used to obtain exclusive products and services.
  • Event Participation: TennisCoin holders can participate in exclusive events, workshops, and competitions organized worldwide. This helps to build a global network of tennis enthusiasts and supporters of the project.

3. Transparency

  • Transparency: All transactions and token distribution will be fully transparent and verifiable on the blockchain. Users will have access to detailed reports on fund status and project activities.
  • Security: The security of tokens and transactions is a top priority. We will employ best security practices to ensure funds are protected and the system is resistant to attacks and vulnerabilities.

Conclusions

The TennisCoin Tokenomics is designed to create a sustainable and growth-oriented ecosystem, promoting inclusion and support for young tennis talent. We are committed to ensuring that every TennisCoin contributes to our goal of making tennis accessible to all and building a global community of enthusiasts and supporters.

Fee Structure

Original Fee Structure:

  • 3% of each transaction is added to the liquidity pool.
  • 2% of each transaction is distributed to token holders.
  • 1% of each transaction is sent to the receive address.

Fee Breakdown:

  • Rewards to Holders: 2%
  • Liquidity Tax: 3%
  • Transaction Tax: 1%

Updated Fee Structure:

  • 1% is the total transaction tax.
  • 2% is burned (removed from circulation).
  • 2% is distributed to token holders.

Example Transaction:

If a user sends 100 tokens:

  • 94 tokens are received by the recipient.
  • 1 tokens are sent to the receive address.
  • 2 tokens are burned.
  • 3 tokens are distributed to token holders.

Updated Fee Breakdown:

  • Rewards to Holders: 2%
  • Burn Tax: 2%
  • Transaction Tax: 1%
